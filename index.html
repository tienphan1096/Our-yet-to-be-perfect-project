What is ResQ?

ResQ stands for "Respond Quick"

Who is behind ResQ?

We're a group of 6 tremendously inspired to-be developers wishing to connect the people of Calgary & surrounding area

What is ResQ's mission?

ResQ's mission is to connect people in times of needs. ResQ wishes to offer Calgarians an intuitive, responsive network of users who are willing to help one another through hard times, i.e heavy snow, vehicle broke-down
